Why Choose Us

Our Strength

  • Quality

    We believe in the power of simple, safe play. Our handcrafted toys are free from plastic and batteries, and made with sustainable Baltic birch wood

  • Eco-Friendly

    At Little Bird Toys, we are committed to eco-friendly practices. Our wooden toys are designed for longevity, and our manufacturing process minimizes waste and supports the US economy.

  • Creative

    Little Toy Bird's toys are designed to inspire imaginative and creative play. Free from batteries and screens, these toys encourage children to engage their creativity, develop motor skills, and enjoy endless hours of open-ended play.

  • Unique

    Little Toy Bird prides itself on offering beautifully handcrafted wooden toys made with attention to detail and superior craftsmanship. Each toy is meticulously designed and built to last, providing children with durable, high-quality playthings.
